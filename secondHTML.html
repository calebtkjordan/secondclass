How to Make a Sandwich

  1. Gather your ingredients: bread, butter, lettuce, tomato, cheese, and ham.
  2. Spread butter on two slices of bread.
  3. Place a slice of cheese on one slice of bread.
  4. Add ham on top of the cheese.
  5. Place lettuce and tomato slices on top of the ham.
  6. Cover with the second slice of bread.
  7. Cut the sandwich in half, if desired.
  8. Enjoy your sandwich!
